Neighborhood

My neighborhood is very small. I live in a town of about 30,000 people. We all know each other for the most part. my favorite thing about living in this small town in my neighborhood is that we can have our own space. I live in a small suburb of this town and it is great to go out four-wheeling and shooting. my neighborhood is the true definition of what it means to be from the midwest.
